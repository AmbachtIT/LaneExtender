[h2]Current limitations[/h2]
Even with Node Controller, manipulating the node system is not trivial. Therefore, there are a few limitations to keep everything a stable as possible. In the future, I might be able to take away some of these limitations.
[list]
[*] The mod only works with vanilla highway assets.
[*] The mod does not work on intersections.
[/list]
